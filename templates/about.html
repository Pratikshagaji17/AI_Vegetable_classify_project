About Us

Green Classify is a web-based vegetable classification system that uses deep learning and computer vision to identify vegetables from images. Users can upload an image of a vegetable, and the system will instantly predict its name using a trained Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model. Designed to support educational, agricultural, and retail applications, Green Classify aims to make vegetable recognition fast, simple, and accessible for everyone.
